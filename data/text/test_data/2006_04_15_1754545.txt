If you are among the 47 million Americans -- a third of all taxpayers -- who push up against the deadline to file their income taxes, we know what you'll be doing this weekend.
You've got to sift through documents stuffed in shoe boxes, file folders, paper bags or just left in a pile who-knows-where to prepare your return or file an extension by the deadline Monday or Tuesday. All the while, you'll be grumbling to yourself, ''There's got to be a better way.''
