No one ever questioned the integrity of Sallie Krawcheck's research reports.
As an analyst at Sanford C. Bernstein, a boutique New York brokerage firm that caters to big investors, Ms. Krawcheck was three times named the top analyst covering the securities industry in an annual poll conducted by Institutional Investor magazine. Just five years after joining Bernstein, Ms. Krawcheck became the firm's director of research. Two years after that, she became its chief executive.
