For the last few months, Peter Bacanovic has been hiding in Los Angeles in a concerted attempt to duck the media and their relentless interest in his role as Martha Stewart's stockbroker.
On his occasional trips to New York, he has avoided his town house on East 70th Street and has been a perpetual house guest among his wide circle of friends in New York society. To keep his mind off his legal troubles, those friends have been holding intimate dinner parties, mostly in their apartments.
