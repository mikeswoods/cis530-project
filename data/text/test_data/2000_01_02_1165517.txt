THINK of this past Christmas as the New Hampshire primary of electronic commerce. The race is far from over, but it was the first high-stakes match. Now the winners are relishing their momentum and the losers are licking their wounds, rethinking their strategies and considering whether to drop out altogether.
The actual returns will dribble in over the next month or two as the key players announce their fourth-quarter earnings. Some Web sites that are private or part of bigger companies will never announce results.
